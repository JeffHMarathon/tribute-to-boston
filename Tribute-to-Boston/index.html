Discography for Boston

  • 1976 - Boston - The debut album, released on August 25, 1976, ranks as one of the best-selling debut albums in U.S. history with over 17 million copies sold.
  • 1978 - Don't look back - Sold over 7 million records.
  • 1986 - Third Stage - The album sold over 4 million copies. The lead single "Amanda" went to No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100
  • 1994 - Walk on - certified platinum by the RIAA, and reached No. 7 on the Billboard Top 200 Albums chart.
  • 2002 - Corporate America - released on the independent label Artemis Records. featuring the largest Boston lineup ever
  • 2013 - Life, Love & Hope - The last recordings of Brad Delp before his death.
